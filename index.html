Hack Club

Hack Club is a global nonprofit network of high school coding clubs where students learn to code by building projects and collaborating with peers. Founded in 2014 by Zach Latta, Hack Club aims to empower high school students to become creators through coding and technology. It has grown to include over 500 clubs and 40,000 students worldwide, providing a supportive environment for young makers and coders.
